Did you know? You can have a floating Google Keep popup, detached from the menu bar, by right-clicking on the menu bar icon and choosing Open Google Keep in a floating popup.

If you don't see any of your Google Accounts listed here it means that there might be a bug, so please scroll down to the page, click Show System Logs and follow the instructions.

Operations

If you don't see your account in the above "Accounts" section, click the button below to start an account discovery. This operation is likely to succeed in case you've just added a new Google Account, otherwise there might be a different problem.

Note: Google Accounts discovery is automatically performed every 2 hours.
